Hiking the New Hampshire Mountains - Each of the Four Seasons

This is my first project for Udacity, and I thought I would share some of the photographs that I’ve taken. Several years ago I began hiking in the White Mountains of New Hampshire, and it’s become a passion of mine. I’ve enjoyed the natural beauty of the mountains, the friendships from the trail and the solitude of the forest. Every time I hike, I try to take at least a few pictures of the people and places.
